# Passive Income Collection Guide

## Revenue Streams

Your skill stack generates income through multiple channels:

### 1. Direct Sales (Primary)

**Platforms:**
- **Gumroad** — Recommended (lowest fees, instant payouts)
- **Your own website** — Full control, higher margins
- **ClawHub** — If they add marketplace payments in future

**How it works:**
1. Customer visits your Gumroad/website
2. Purchases skill bundle ($49, $79, or $119)
3. Payment processed instantly (Stripe/PayPal)
4. Funds deposited to your bank account

**Payout schedules:**
- Gumroad: Weekly (every Friday) or instant (for $2 fee)
- Stripe: 2-day rolling basis
- PayPal: Instant

### 2. Service Upsells (Highest Margin)

**Offer these services to buyers:**

| Service | Price | Time Required |
|---------|-------|---------------|
| Setup & Configuration | $200-500 | 2-4 hours |

| Integration Service | $300-800 | 3-6 hours |
| Monthly Support | $100-300/mo | 1-2 hrs/mo |

**How to offer:**
- Add upsell on Gumroad thank-you page

## Setting Up Payment Collection

### Option A: Gumroad (Easiest)

**Setup steps:**

1. **Create account:** https://gumroad.com/signup
2. **Connect payment:**
   - Stripe (recommended for US)
   - PayPal (global)
   - Payoneer (for international)
3. **Create products:**
   - Product 1: Content Creator Suite ($49)
   - Product 2: Freelancer Revenue Engine ($79)
   - Product 3: Complete Stack ($119)
4. **Upload files:** Attach `.skill` files to each product
5. **Set thank-you page:** Add service upsell offer
6. **Publish:** Make products live

**Gumroad fees:**
- Free plan: 10% + processing fees
- Premium ($10/mo): 0% + processing fees

**Break-even:** Premium pays for itself at ~$100/month in sales

### Option B: Stripe + Own Website

**More work, higher margins:**

1. **Create Stripe account:** https://stripe.com
2. **Add payment form to website:**
   - Use Stripe Checkout (hosted)
   - Or Stripe Elements (embedded)
3. **Deliver files after payment:**
   - Email with download link
   - Or redirect to download page
4. **Handle taxes:** Stripe Tax or TaxJar

**Stripe fees:**
- 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
- No monthly fee

**Example on $119 sale:**
- Customer pays: $119
- Stripe fee: $3.75
- You receive: $115.25

### Option C: PayPal

**Best for international:**

1. **Create business account:** https://paypal.com/business
2. **Create payment buttons:**
   - Go to Tools → All Tools → PayPal Buttons
   - Create 3 buttons for your bundles
3. **Embed on website:** Copy/paste button code
4. **Auto-deliver files:** Use PayPal IPN webhook

**PayPal fees:**
- 2.9% + $0.30 (domestic)
- 4.4% + fixed fee (international)

## Tax Considerations

### Record Keeping

Track:
- All sales (date, amount, customer)
- Refunds
- Fees (Gumroad, Stripe, PayPal)
- Expenses (domain, hosting, tools)

**Tools:**
- QuickBooks Self-Employed
- Wave (free)
- Spreadsheet (simplest)

### Quarterly Taxes

If US-based:
- Pay estimated taxes quarterly (April, June, Sept, Jan)
- 15.3% self-employment tax
- Income tax on profits

**Simplify:**
- Set aside 25-30% of revenue for taxes
- Use QuickBooks for quarterly estimates
- Hire accountant if >$50k/year
